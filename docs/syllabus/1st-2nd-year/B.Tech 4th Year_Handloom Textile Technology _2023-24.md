| KHT071   | Technical Textiles   | 3L:0T:0P   | 3Credits   |
|----------|----------------------|------------|------------|

## Objective:

1. To enable the students to learn about the concept of technical textiles and filtration through textiles.
2. To  enable  the  students  to  know  about  the  concept  of  geo  and  medical textiles and their application.
3. To enable the students to know  about protective textiles and their application in different sectors.
4. To enable the students to know about sports and automotive textiles.
5. To  enable  the  students  to  know  about  the  swing  threads,  cords  and  ropes used in technical textiles.

| Unit   | Topics                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |   Lectures |
|--------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| I      | Introduction: Definition and scope for technical textiles, brief idea about technical fibers, role ofyarn and fabric construction, composite material. Filtration textiles: Definition of filtration parameters, theory of dust collection and solid liquid separation, filtration requirements, concept of pore size and particle size, role of fiber, fabric construction and finishing treatments. |          8 |
| II     | Geotextiles: Brief idea about geosynthetics and their uses, essential properties of geotextiles, geotextile testing and evaluation, application examples of geotextiles. Medical textiles : Classification of medical textiles, description of different medical textiles.                                                                                                                            |          8 |
| III    | Protective Clothing : Brief idea about different type of protective clothing, functional requirement of textiles in defense including ballistic protection materials and parachute cloth, temperature and flame retardant clothing, chemical protective clothing, water proof breathable fabrics.                                                                                                     |          8 |

| IV   | Sports and recreation textiles : Functional requirement of different type of product and their construction. Automotive Textiles : Application of textiles in automobiles, requirement and design for different tyres, airbags and belts, methods of production and properties of textiles used in these applications.   | 8                             |
|------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| V    | Sewing threads, cords and ropes : Types, method of production and applications, requirements, structure and properties. Other uses of technical textile: requirements and types of textiles used for paper making, agricultural, electronics, transmission belting, hoses, canvas covers and tarpaulins.                 | functional Functional power 8 |

## Outcomes:

Upon completion of the course, the students will be able: -

1. To  know  about  the  concept  of  technical  textiles  and  filtration  through textiles.
2. To know about the concept of geo &amp; medical textiles and their application.
3. To know about protective textiles and their application in different sectors.
4. To know about Sports &amp;automotive textiles and their applications.
5. To know about the basic of swing threads, cords and ropes used in technical textiles.

| KHT072   | Computer Aided Textile Design (CATD)   | 3L: 0T :0P   | 3 Credits   |
|----------|----------------------------------------|--------------|-------------|

## Objective:

1. To enable the students to learn about the fundamentals of CATD tools.
2. To enable the students to learn about transfer of designs on to point paper and design calculation.
3. To  enable  the  students  to  learn  about  application  of  CATD  in  dobby  and  jacquard designing.
4. To enable the students to learn about motifs setting, colour masking and protecting.
5. To enable the students to learn about computer aided punching and colour forecasting.

| Unit   | Topics                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |   Lectures |
|--------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| I      | Introduction of computer application in textile designing - fundamentals- different methods and advantages of CATD.Basics of computer designing, colours and colour palette. Colour theory of monitor and printers, importance of CATD.                                                                                                                                                                                         |          8 |
| II     | Transfer of designs to point paper- various steps in transferring designs from sketch to point paper. Design calculation as per given jacquard and harness set - pixel - resolution and its relation with threads and threads per inch. Vector and raster images - X and Y in designing - different drawing tools.                                                                                                              |          8 |
| III    | Yarn - weave creation - creation of yarn library with different types - counts and quality - creation of weaves and saving. Dobby Designing - creation - design, draft, peg plan creation and its manipulation, colour patterns, simulation, print out in real scale.Jacquard designing - principles of creating motifs in computer - drawing tools.                                                                            |          8 |
| IV     | Motif Scanning - scanning parameters - colours and attributes, editing the image for graph making - scaling, rotating, reversing, dropping etc. - Importance of colour application in motif - colour masking and protecting (picking of one colour from more colours - protecting of a colour from other colours); Repeat setting to see the joining; Methods of creating different styles and forms of designs using Computer. |          8 |
| V      | Computer aided card punching - semi automatic and automatic punching machines; Printing Designs - usages of CATD in Textile Printing Colour Forecasting and Fashion Designing; Colour selection as per the changing Fashion; Colour forecast for the export fabric as per the global trend. Measures for contemporary designing in Handloom Sector and related fashion aspects according to the International Fashion Trade.    |          8 |

## Outcomes:

Upon completion of the course the student will be able to: -

1. Understand about the fundamentals of CATD tools.
2. Understand about transfer of designs on to point paper and design calculation.
3. Understand about application of CATD in dobby and jacquard designing.
4. Understand about motifs scanning, colour masking and protecting.
5. Understand about the computer aided punching and colour forecasting.

| KHT075   | Characteristics of Polymers and fibers   | 3L: 0T :0P   | 3 Credits   |
|----------|------------------------------------------|--------------|-------------|

## Objectives:

1. To enable the students to understand the morphological characteristics of fiber and transmission and Scanning electron microscopes-principle &amp; different techniques
2. To enable the students to understand the moisture absorption characteristics of fiber.
3. To enable the students to understand the Tensile characteristics
4. To enable the students to understand the opticalandfrictional characteristicsof fiber.
5. To enable the students to understandthermal characteristics of fiber.

| Unit   | Topics                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |   Lectures |
|--------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| I      | Structure and Morphology- Classification of fibres; study of morphological structures of fibers; physical properties of fibres. order and disorder in fibre structure; molecular conformations - planar zig-zag, helical, and lamellar conformations.                                                                                    |          7 |
| II     | Transmission and Scanning electron microscopes -principle; construction and working; X-ray diffraction techniques - estimation of crystallinity and orientation. In frared radiation and dichroism techniques; chemical element and group identification by transmittance and optical density methods, molecular orientation estimation. |          8 |
| III    | Moisture Absorption Characteristics- Theories of moisture sorption; Moisture absorption behavior of natural and man-made fibres; influence of fibre structure, humidity and temperature on the moisture absorption. Heat of sorption - integral and differential, factors influencing heat of sorption.                                  |          9 |
| IV     | Optical and Frictional Characteristics -Reflexion and Lustre -objective and subjective methods of measurement - refractive index and its measurement - birefringence, factors influencing birefringence - Absorption and dichroism Friction static, directional friction in wool - friction.                                             |          9 |
| V      | Thermal Characteristics- Thermal transitions of fibres - thermal conductivity, thermal expansion and contraction, Glass transition temperature (Tg) melting temperature (Tm),Static electricity in textile fibres                                                                                                                        |          7 |

## Outcomes:

Upon completion of this course, the student shall be able to

1. To understand the morphological characteristics of different fibers.
2. To understand the moisture absorption characteristics of different fibers.
3. Choose appropriate fiber for the required property
4. To understand the optical and frictional characteristics of different fibers.
5. To understand thermal characteristics of different fibers.

## Statement of Procedure of

## Minor Degree for B.Tech. Students

[As approved by the Academic Council of the University in its 67 th Meeting, Agenda Point 67.10 ]

## 1. Purpose:

- (i) To give opportunity to the students to acquire a domain knowledge in certain area that is  NOT  RELATED  to  his/her  Major  Discipline  to  pursue  his/her  additional  allied academic interest in contemporary area.
- (ii) To  gain  skills  to  pursue  interdisciplinary  research  and  increase  the  employability quotient.

## 2. Minor degree:

- (i) A  minor  degree  can  be  acquired  by  pursuing  any  one  of  the  many  Minor  Tracks offered by the University
- (ii) A  Minor  Track  is  a  defined  as  an ORDERED SEQUENCE of Five  (5)  courses constituting 18-20 credits aimed to give a reasonable breadth and depth to the student in certain domain NOT related to the major discipline.
- (iii) Minor Track courses will be spread from 4 th to 8 th semester of the B.Tech. Program.
- (iv) Credits  earned  through  Minor  Track  will  be  in  addition  to  the  essential  Credits (&gt;=160 Credits) to be earned for Major discipline of the regular B.Tech. Program.

## 3. Status of AICTE Approval

University  may  offer  AICTE  suggested  Minor  tracks  or  may  design  their  own  Minor Tracks. No separate approval is required from AICTE for Minor degree.

( Reference: Approval Process Handbook 2021-22, Chapter VII)

## 4. Eligibility for Registration:

- (i) Registration to the Minor Track will be done in 4 th Semester provided interested students possess  a YGPA of 6.5 or above upto FIRST YEAR with  all  courses  passed  in  one attempt  without  grace.  In  case  of Lateral  Entry, he/she would  be  required  to  have minimum CGPA of 6.5 or 65 percent marks, as the case may be, in his/her entry level qualifying degree.
- (ii) One can opt to register for maximum ONLY ONE Track (out of all Minor Tracks and Honors Tracks) or None.

## 5. Example Minor Degree Name:

B.Tech. in Civil Engineering with minor degree in Data Science

## 6. Award of B. Tech. with Minor Degree :

- (i) One will be eligible for award of Minor Degree, if he completes all the courses of chosen Minor Track.
- (ii) There will be no transfer of credit from Minor track to regular B.Tech degree Course of major discipline or vice-versa.
- (iii) All the courses of Minor Track have to be completed in one attempt.
- (iv) If  a  student,  who  has  registered  for  Minor  Track,  fails  in  any  of  the  course  of  Minor Track, his registration for Minor Track will cease to be active. Such a student will not be eligible for award of the Minor degree. However, he/she will continue to be eligible for award of his / her regular B.Tech. Degree.
- (v) Moreover, in such cases where one is not able to complete the Minor track, the credits earned by the students through the minor track will be recorded as Extra Credit on the mark sheet.
- (vi) One can graduate with Minor degree only if he/she fulfills the requirement for award of the his/her regular B.Tech. degree in his/her major discipline.
- (vii) The Minor degree must be simultaneously completed along with major degree. A student can not earn minor degree later, if he/she has  already completed and earned his major degree.

- (viii) Registration to the Minor degree will be valid only if registration to Major Discipline of Regular B. Tech. degree exists. If one drops the registration to regular B.Tech. Program or completes the regular B.Tech. Program, he/she will not be allowed to pursue his/her minor degree program.
- (ix) CGPA  of  the  major  degree  and  minor  degree  would  be  calculated  separately  and mentioned on the Mark Sheet separately. The CGPA obtained in the major degree would be treated as CGPA of the program without including accumulated grades from Minor track. The division will be awarded based upon the performance in the regular B. Tech. program only.

## 7. Registration Fee:

No additional Tuition Fee will be charged from the students registering for Minor degree. Although, only a nominal token fee (non-refundable) of Rs 100=00 per semester must be charged to confirm the active registration in each of the semester having minor course.

## 8. Record Keeping:

The institute and the University shall maintain the detailed record (minor program wise as well as parent branch wise) of the students enrolled for the Minor degree.

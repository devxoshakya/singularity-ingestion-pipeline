## Syllabus

| SUBJECT CODE: HTDT 401       | COURSE TITLE: DAIRY EQUIPMENT &MACHINERY   |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| EXAMINATION DURATION: 3 Hrs. | SEMESTER: IV                               |

OBJECTIVE: This  course  provides  a  comprehensive  understanding  of  the  science,  technology,  and working  principles  of  equipments  involved  in  the  dairy  sector  along  with  their  utility,  merits  and limitations .

COURSE OUTCOME: After successful completion of the course the students will be able to have:

- Exposure to modern dairy machinery and equipment
- Ability to operate and maintain dairy machinery and equipment
- Ability to plan, organize, prioritize, calculate, concentrate and handle equipments and operations.
- Knowledge regarding advancements in automation, process optimization, and product innovation.

| SUBJECT CODE:HTDT 501       | COURSE TITLE: MILK PROCUREMENT, PROCESSING AND PACKAGING   |
|-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| EXAMINATION DURATION: 3 Hrs | SEMESTER: V                                                |
|                             | PRE-REQUISITE : NIL                                        |

## OBJECTIVE: To provide knowledge about:

- Dairy plant layout, dairy cooperative system and milk procurement.
- Composition, physicochemical properties and chemistry of milk.
- Microbiology of milk and hygiene &amp; sanitation of dairy equipment.
- Processing and packaging of milk
- Byproduct utilization and waste management in dairy.

## COURSE OUTCOME: After successful completion of the course the students will be able to:

- Understand the dairy cooperative system and methods of milk procurement.
- Composition and basic chemistry of milk.
- Role of microorganisms in dairy and &amp; methods of cleaning and sanitization in dairy industry.
- Importance of different processing steps for fluid milk and special milks; Packaging materials and forms for dairy products: Utilization of byproducts and management of waste.

OBJECTIVE: To  provide  basic  knowledge  about  the  various  aspects  involved  in  dairy  management  and entrepreneurship with an aim to build entrepreneurial competency among youth to sustain dairy business and attract them towards dairy sector
